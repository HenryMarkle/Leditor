## Drizzle Update (Part Two)

- Added custom rope/long prop support.
 - Custom ropes/longs follow the same format as their reqular counterparts. 
 - To add custom ropes, append your custom rope into the `Props/Init.txt` with the following format: `[#nm:"Wire", #tp:"rope", #depth:0, #tags:[], #notes:[], #segmentLength:3, #collisionDepth:0, #segRad:1.0, #grav:0.5, #friction:0.5, #airFric:0.9, #stiff:0, #previewColor:color(255,0, 0), #previewEvery:4, #edgeDirection:0.0, #rigid:0.0, #selfPush:0.0, #sourcePush:0.0]`
 - To add custom longs, append your custom long into the `Props/Init.txt` with the following format: `[#nm:"Stretched Pipe", #tp:"long", #depth:0, #tags:[], #notes:[]]`
 - Texture follow the same format as in the `Cast` folder, however, the editor does not care about them pre-rendering.
 - This feature has not been tested.
